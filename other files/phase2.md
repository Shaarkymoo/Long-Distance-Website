1. Evolution Pet
A shared creature that never dies or requires care. Every interaction gives it Evolution Points (EP), allowing it to level up and unlock new physical traits, personalities, titles, and behaviors. It can explore the world, find items, and record memorable events in its timeline.

The fun comes from seeing how bizarre and unique the creature becomes over hundreds of interactions. Rather than maintaining a pet, you're watching a species evolve.

Core mechanics

Shared level and EP
Permanent evolution traits
Inventory of discovered items
Random events and thoughts
Exploration missions
Timeline of milestones
Hidden evolution combinations


🌍 2. Shared World

Instead of a traditional garden, both of you build a small civilization together.

Each visit lets you perform one action—plant a forest, construct a building, research a technology, celebrate a festival, or explore the wilderness. These actions permanently affect the world's statistics, unlock new eras, and create a history of how the civilization developed.

The world never resets. Every click becomes part of its history.

Core mechanics

Persistent world statistics
Eras that unlock naturally
Buildings, technologies and discoveries
Hidden unlock combinations
Random world events
Shared history log
Individual contribution statistics



3. Expeditions (Quest Board)

An infinitely replayable challenge generator.

The website creates a procedural photography challenge using combinations of predefined prompts. Both players submit one photo whenever they're able. After both submissions are in, each player reviews the other's photo and votes on whether it completed the challenge, with an optional "favorite" vote for a small bonus.

There are no deadlines, no automatic judging, and no pressure—just shared creativity and friendly competition.

Core mechanics

Procedurally generated challenges
One submission per player
Mutual review and voting
XP rewards
Trophy cabinet
Complete expedition archive
Secret rare expeditions


4. Museum of Curiosities

A permanent collection game.

Instead of random uploads, every photograph belongs to an exhibit and a specific slot within that exhibit (for example, "Architecture → Doors" or "Patterns → Symmetry"). Once a slot has an accepted image, it remains part of the museum forever, while future uploads become alternate entries.

The goal is to slowly complete exhibits while competing for Curator Picks and achievements.

Core mechanics

Permanent exhibits
Collectible slots
Alternate entries
Curator Picks
Museum completion percentage
Hidden exhibits
Achievement system


The Archive

A collaborative discovery system.

Rather than uploading anything, players send the Evolution Pet to different locations such as forests, ruins, mountains, libraries or oceans. The pet returns with discoveries: creatures, artifacts, plants, places, books, mysteries or collectible items.

Every discovery permanently fills out a shared encyclopedia. Duplicate discoveries become Knowledge, which can unlock hints, hidden areas, or future content.

This acts as the exploration engine that ties the entire website together.

Core mechanics

Area-based exploration
Weighted random discoveries
Collectible encyclopedia
Discovery chains
Knowledge currency
Hidden areas
Exploration history
